UX Designer

Portland, OR

SabiKerja is a curated headhunter agency based in Jakarta that is well-known as a one-stop talent solution in which we connect curated quality talent with the institution or company that partners with us.

We are seeking a talented UX Designer to craft exceptional user experiences for our digital products. In this role, you will work closely with cross-functional teams, including product managers, developers, and UI designers, to translate user needs and business goals into intuitive and engaging designs.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Conduct user research through interviews, surveys, and usability testing to understand user needs and behaviors.
  • Analyze research findings to create user personas, journey maps, and use cases.
  • Develop wireframes, storyboards, and prototypes to visualize design solutions.
  • Collaborate with UI designers to ensure seamless integration of aesthetics and functionality.
  • Work with product and development teams to align designs with business and technical requirements.
  • Continuously evaluate and iterate on designs based on user feedback and analytics.
  • Advocate for user-centered design principles across the organization.

Job Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, or related field.
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in account management within the IT industry.
  • Proven experience as a UX Designer with a strong portfolio showcasing design processes and outcomes.
  • Strong communication and negotiation skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Excellent problem-solving and decision-making abilities.
