Marketing Coordinator

San Francisco, CA

SabiKerja is a curated headhunter agency based in Jakarta that is well-known as a one-stop talent solution in which we connect curated quality talent with the institution or company that partners with us.

As a Marketing Coordinator, you will play a pivotal role in supporting the marketing team by planning, executing, and monitoring marketing campaigns across various channels. This includes coordinating promotional activities, managing content creation, analyzing market trends, and ensuring brand consistency in all marketing materials. You will work closely with internal teams and external vendors to ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Plan and execute marketing campaigns across various channels, including digital, print, and social media.
  • Collaborate with the content team to produce engaging and consistent marketing materials.
  • Organize and coordinate marketing events, trade shows, and product launches.
  • Conduct market research to identify trends, customer preferences, and competitive insights.
  • Work with sales, design, and product teams to ensure cohesive messaging and branding.
  • Assist in managing marketing budgets and ensuring cost-effective use of resources.

Job Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing Communication, Management, or related field.
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in account management within the IT industry.
  • Proven track record of successfully managing government projects.
  • Strong communication and negotiation skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Excellent problem-solving and decision-making abilities.
